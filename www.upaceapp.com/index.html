Who We Are

Get to know us.

In college I hated going to the university recreation center… waiting up to 30 minutes for a cardio machine, needing to be at the gym 40 minutes in advance for yoga, and having to walk out of the gym since its simply too crowded.

I thought there has to be a better option for students. But after researching other university facilities I realized there wasn’t.

I have spoken to over 30 universities and hundreds of students across the United States to find out what students & their administration want in their fitness app.

That's how the upace journey began, and that's how it will stay:

Made By You, For You.

Founder Rachel Koretsky
